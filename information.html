Types of Mental Health Disorders

Mental health disorders, also known as mental illnesses or psychiatric disorders, encompass a wide range of conditions that affect a person's thoughts, emotions, behavior, and overall well-being.

Anxiety Disorder

Everfelt like your worries were taking over? That might be anxiety, where everyday situations can feel overwhelming. It's like having a constant companion offear, even in the safest moments.

Major Depression

Have you ever experienced days where even getting out of bed feels like climbing a mountain? That's what major depression can feel, like it's a heavy fog that dims the joy in everything, leaving you feeling lost and worthless.

Behavioral Disorder

Sometimes our actions can feel out of control, like we're on autopilot. That's what happens with behavioral disorders, where things like anger, impulsivity, or self-destructive behavior take the wheel.

Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)

ADHD

Do you ever struggle to focus, like your brain is a never-ending whirl wind of thoughts? That's the challenge of ADHD, where concentration feels like catching smoke, and sitting still is next to impossible.

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

PTSD

Have you ever experienced something so terrifying that it feels like it's haunting you? That's PTSD, a relentless storm of nightmares, flashbacks, and anxiety triggered by memories of a traumatic event.
